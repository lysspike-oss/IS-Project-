Getting Started with Crochet

Crocheting is a relaxing handcraft that turns yarn into beautiful, functional pieces. Start with simple stitches and enjoy making scarves, blankets, and gifts.

Steps to Start Crocheting

  1. Choose your yarn and hook size.
  2. Learn the basic stitches:
    • Slip Stitch
    • Chain
    • Single
    • Double
  3. Practice a foundation chain.
  4. Create your first small swatch.
  5. Finish with a neat edge and weave in ends.
